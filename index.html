MERGE INDUSTRIES

What is Merge?

Merge Industries is a software development company setup in 2016 by a group of Ghanaian developers from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). The company started out with 9 individuals in 2016 and is now made up of about 350 personnel that consists of software developers, software testers, project managers, technical leads, product owners, user acceptance testers, project sponsors and subject matter experts aimed at meeting the software needs of both private and public companies. Merge is now and outsourcing company with over 25 branches worldwide. The leading branches are those situated in Ghana, United States of America and in South Africa which have the largest target markets compared to the other branches. Merge Industries typically specializes in building operating system software as well as application software.
